The Launceston Esk Market is the largest market in Northern Tasmania, 150 undercover stalls and even more outside. Stalls including hot food and cold refreshments, fresh fruit and vegetables, collectable comics, bric-a- brac, pre-loved items, clothing, books, tools, collectable items, craft items, knitting, sewing, beads, wooden and rustic furniture, novelties and toys and even a Jumping Castle for the children.
Only 2 minutes walk from carpark to QVMAG and Tram Museum, on AFL days drive in and turn left to park your car with us and enjoy the market. Have something to eat and drink and then go to the footy with no parking fees.
